Where do senior citizens in Singapore reside?

In Singapore, senior citizens are commonly defined as Singapore citizens aged 65 years old and above.
In 2022, 18.4% of Singapore citizens are senior citizens (The Straits Times , 2022).
From the visualisation below, we can observe that Tampines East subzone has the most senior citizens, and that senior citizen count in the Eastern subzone is the highest.
